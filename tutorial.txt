To use the insert mode in vim, press 'i' or 'a'

Capital Y copies a line of text to the buffer!
Capital Y copies a line of text to the buffer!

Capital P pastes the copied line to the current position!

dd will delete a whole line of text! It also allows the deleted line to be pasted.

yy copies a whole line of text!!
yy copies a whole line of text!!

:w allows you to save a file

:wq saves the file and quits vim!!
